Quality Assurance - Benefits

For every purchase you make, we guarntee that our Bees will be happy, healthy and loved.

  • Our Bees have a good source of fresh clean water
  • Our Bees are free-range and are not exposed to pesticides
  • Our Honey is handcrafted by our Bee's and is 100% Organic
  • Many people with seasonal allergies get some relief with local honey
  • Our Honey is not pasteurized, and retains all of its natural enzymes, vitamins, and minerals

Hive locations are managed throughout the year and during winter the Beekeeper places special pollen/nectar food packages inside the hives to feed them.
